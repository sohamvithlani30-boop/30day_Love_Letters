The Beginning (2019)

It feels like yesterday. Late 2019, 10:30 PM.

You asked the question, and my mind went blank. I didn't know what to say because I couldn't believe the girl of my dreams was choosing me.

I remember the silent video calls where we couldn't speak because your mom was around, but your eyes said everything. I remember the air kisses through the screen that felt more real than anything else.

We were magic then.

The Anchor (0730)

But love isn't always a straight line.

We walked through the silence. 1.5 years of being blocked. 1.5 years of me wondering if I would ever hear your voice again.

On May 30th, 2025, I did something desperate. I tattooed 0730 on my skin.

It wasn't just ink. It was a spell to bring you back. It was me screaming to the universe that I belong to you. You came back, but the pain came too. Seeing you with others broke me daily, but I took every piece of heartbreak just to stay close to you.

My Apology (2025)

I know 2025 wasn't perfect.

I know I irritated you. I know sometimes my love feels heavy, overwhelming, or 'too much.' I am sorry for every time I made you angry when I only wanted to make you smile.

I am sorry for the chaos. But please know this: Even if my methods were wrong, my intention was always pure.

If a thousand tears of mine can buy just one small smile on your face... then every tear was worth it.
